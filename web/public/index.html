How to request and bump

Request a song in chat: !request Artist - Title or paste a YouTube link.

To bump a song use: !prio after requesting.

The first subscriber request each stream is bumped for free. Followers, raids, cheers of ≥200 bits, and 1 point per 5 gifted subs grant priority points.

This page updates live. Prioritized songs are highlighted.

Tip: Click a title to open its YouTube link.
